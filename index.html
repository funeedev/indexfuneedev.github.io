About Lakota Nexus

A digital gathering place for the Lakota language and its learners.

Lakota Nexus was built as a digital gathering place — a nexus — for the Lakota language and those who wish to learn it. Like stars in a constellation, each word connects to something greater. This platform honors the Očhéthi Šakówiŋ and their living language by making it accessible, searchable, and alive for every learner.

Language

Lakota (Lak̇ȟótiyapi) is a Siouan language spoken by the Lakota people of the Great Plains. It is one of the three major dialects of the Sioux language, alongside Dakota and Nakota. The language features complex verb morphology, evidential markers, and a rich vocabulary for the natural world.

Our Approach

Every entry is sourced with care and respect for the communities who carry this language. We are committed to accuracy, cultural sensitivity, and ongoing collaboration with Lakota speakers and educators. This platform is a living project — always growing.

API & Data

Lakota Nexus is built with an open API architecture. Translation and dictionary data are served from a community-maintained backend, making it possible for developers and educators to build on top of this resource.
